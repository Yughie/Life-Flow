Congratulations on making the important decision to sign up as an organ, eye and tissue donor! Your decision can have a huge impact on the lives of others.
Before going forward, did you already agree on donating your organ on your driver's license?
Please fill out the form to register as an organ, eye, and tissue donor. Fields marked with an * are required fields. When you register as an organ, eye, and tissue donor, your registration remains in effect until you revoke it. Please be sure to complete all the steps in the process before leaving the site. After you register, you will receive an email confirming your registration as well as an email you may forward to family and friends. Filipino residents must reaffirm the decision to be a donor through the appropriate legal channels. Signing up as a donor means you are making the decision to save lives by donating your organs and tissues at the time of your death.
