Is There an Age Limit for Organ Donation

There's no age limit to donation or to signing up. People in their 50s, 60s, 70s, and older have donated and received organs.

Organ Donation and Children

Religion and Organ Donation

Many religions in the United States (U.S.) support organ donation. The decision to become a donor is personal. If you have questions about your religion and organ donation, ask your faith leader.
    Religions that support organ donation include:
  • Disciples of Christ
  • Episcopalian
  • Evangelical Covenant Church
  • Islam
  • Judaism
  • Evangelical Lutheran Church in America
  • Mormon Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints
  • Presbyterian
